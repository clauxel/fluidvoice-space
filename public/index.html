Product thesis

Dictation is faster when the setup is already decided.

FluidVoice brings together local-first speech, model choice, Mac permissions, overlays, Write Mode, Command Mode, local history, and optional enhancement. The planner turns those choices into a launch checklist instead of a settings maze.

01

Choose models

Compare zero-download, low-latency, multilingual, Intel-compatible, and high-accuracy paths.

02

Set permissions

Prepare microphone, accessibility, Apple Events, speech recognition, startup, and hotkey guidance.

03

Protect output

Decide when cloud providers, history, analytics, exports, and local enhancement are acceptable.

04

Gate exports

Preview the plan freely; full exports and policy packs route through pricing before API access.
